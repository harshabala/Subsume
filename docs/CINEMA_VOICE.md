# Subsume Cinema Voice Guide

**Register:** A private picture palace and archive — not a tracker, not a spreadsheet.

**Tone:** Calm, literary, humane. Words drawn from cinema's past (celluloid, reel, dossier), present (projection, screening, premiere), and future (anticipated, programme, discoveries yet unspooled).

**Rules:**
- Primary nav stays scannable (Discovery, Archive, Settings) but subtitles and empty states carry the poetry.
- Buttons stay actionable: "Save reflection", "Add to archive" — not jargon-only.
- Settings and errors stay plain; section leads can be cinematic.
- Avoid military/telegraph voice (surveillance, dispatch, rescind) — prefer house-manager / curator voice (programme, enrol, marquee, projection).
- Emotional copy centers the human: what stayed with you, afterglow, resonance, memory.
- No em-dashes in new copy; use commas or middots.

**Lexicon (rotate, do not stack):**
projection · reel · frame · marquee · programme · premiere · auteur · luminary · celluloid · silver screen · picture palace · dossier · ledger · inscription · resonance · afterglow · screening · repertoire · vault · montage · mise-en-scène · anticipated · now showing · house lights
